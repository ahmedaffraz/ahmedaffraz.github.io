Project details

Experience luxury living with our interactive AR walk-through created for Damac Properties. Our team at MOI pays attention to every detail, including lighting, environment, and ultra-realistic high-definition textures. Our interactive Damac visualization eliminates the need to imagine yourself in a space and provides an immersive and believable experience. Among all available solutions, immersive augmented reality (AR) experiences have the greatest potential to inspire buying decisions.

Responsibilities
As a co-developer of the app for both iOS and Android platforms, I played a pivotal role in its development, ensuring compatibility and functionality across multiple operating systems. Additionally, I focused on optimizing the app to run smoothly on low-spec devices without compromising its quality.
