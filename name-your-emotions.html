Project details

Experience "Name Your Emotions" - a virtual reality adventure that explores the love parents have for their children. Developed by Evolve Innovative Solutions in collaboration with Hind Al Oud, this immersive experience is designed for kids aged 7 to 12. Hind Al Oud is well-known for creating boutique fragrances that cater to various emotions. Recently, they offered children an opportunity to name a new emotion and win prizes through a one-of-a-kind VR experience. The VR experience was based on the sacrifices their parents made, taking them on an emotional journey. At the end of the experience, the children named the authentic feeling they experienced and received a panoramic screenshot of their creation via mail.

Responsibilities
As the project lead, I assumed a multifaceted role and had a range of responsibilities. These included designing, developing, planning, troubleshooting, environment lighting, setting up VR experiences in malls, and successfully executing activities related to the project.
