Project details

Discover the convenience of shopping in a VR popup store without leaving your home. With hand-tracking technology, the device can detect your movements in real-time, making it easy for you to interact with products, try them on, and make purchases while on the move.

Responsibilities
As part of my responsibilities, I took charge of creating multiple prototypes and played a key role in developing and improving the user experience. This involved iterating on designs, gathering user feedback, and implementing enhancements to ensure a seamless and satisfying user experience.
