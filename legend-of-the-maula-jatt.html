Project details

The Legend of Maula Jatt is a VR gaming experience that is based on a movie with the same title. This product provides an excellent gaming experience and is utilized for promoting the movie. The story revolves around an epic rivalry between two main characters. In this experience, the player takes on the role of the main character and battles against enemy antagonists using a large, ax-shaped glaive. Our team at MOI achieved exceptional realism in our game by paying close attention to detail. We used in-house photogrammetry scans made from 360° photographic data and motion capture to create all 3D assets, including recording each character's unique gait, dialogue delivery, and signature moves. As a result, the final product features ultra-realistic digital doubles of all characters.

Responsibilities
In this project, I played a crucial role in various aspects, including gameplay programming, AI, level design, audio mixing, environment lighting, and optimization. Additionally, I implemented a pipeline for capturing and recording animations, applying it specifically to the actors' photogrammetry models.
