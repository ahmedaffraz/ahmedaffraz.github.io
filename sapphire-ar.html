Project details

Discover the world's first Augmented Reality app for Eastern wear - Sapphire AR. Browse through the latest collection in 3D on your phone screen, examining each dress up close and from any angle with the 360-degree feature. Once you've found your perfect dress, you can purchase it instantly through the app. The best part is that the app can be used on non-AR compatible devices, as it downloads a 3D model of the dress in real time from the server. With over 350 3D photogrammetry scans, this captivating AR application allows you to view 360-degree videos and 3D models of dresses and make purchases right from the app. Isn't it amazing?
