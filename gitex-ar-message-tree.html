Project details

The Ministry of Immersion introduced a new Immersive Pop-up AR App for GITEX 2020. This app allows users to transform static posters into a dynamic 3D AR experience that can be interacted with on all devices. With this app, users can bring a lifeless poster of a tree to life with just one scan. Additionally, users can write a message on the "Augmented Tree" and send it back in a fun, animated way using only their smartphone.

Responsibilities
In this project, I made significant contributions as an AR developer, backend developer, and ideator. Through these responsibilities, I played a vital role in the project's success by leveraging my skills and expertise in these areas.
