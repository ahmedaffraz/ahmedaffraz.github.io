Project details

Imagine a game where you can express yourself through your own space! Inspired by the post-COVID world where we were all locked in our own rooms (or, as we like to call them, "spaces"), tinySPACES lets you express yourself in a unique way. You'll have hundreds of options for furniture, decor, wallpaper, and floor textures to make your space truly your own. But that's not all - you can also choose background music to fit your mood and visit other players' spaces to see how they've personalized their tinySPACES. Plus, you can send messages to connect with others and build a sense of community in this virtual world. TinySPACES will inspire your creativity and give you a fun way to express yourself. It's the perfect way to unite people and encourage social interaction in a world where we've all been apart for too long. So what are you waiting for? Let's create some tinySPACES and have some fun!

Responsibilities
As a sole developer, I undertook the entire game development process from start to finish. My primary responsibility was handling the game's development and design aspects and testing and integrating the multiplayer functionality. I also contributed to the game design phase, providing input and ideas to shape the game's aesthetics, user interface, and overall player experience.
